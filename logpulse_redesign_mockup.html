Design Notes · Light

Left-border accent lines on every card replace the old "colored chip + text" pattern. Color communicates severity at a glance.

Live pulse animation on the green wordmark dot and healthy service indicators signals real-time connection status.

Warm off-white background (#F7F6F3) vs clinical white. Reduces eye strain for long monitoring sessions.

JetBrains Mono for all data — timestamps, paths, trace IDs, durations. Syne for section headings only.

Design Notes · Dark

GitHub-dark baseline (#0D1117) rather than pure #000. Deep slate feels intentional, not lazy. Surface layers step up in lightness for depth.

Error indicators glow in dark mode. The pulsing red dot on a down service creates genuine urgency that a static icon cannot.

Chart gradients are richer at 35% opacity vs. 20% in light — dark mode allows more dramatic gradient fills without looking washed out.

SWITCH button in the env-switcher uses accent-dim background — critical affordance, not just a text label.
